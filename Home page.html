Meet the Gang

New Zealand White rabbit is one of the most popular and recognizable rabbit breeds in the world. Known for their snow-white coats and bright ruby-red eyes, these rabbits are both striking in appearance and gentle in temperament. They're known for their friendly nature and adaptability, making them wonderful pets for families and individuals alike.

Our rabbits are raised with love and care, ensuring they're healthy, socialized, and ready to become part of your family. Each bunny receives individual attention to develop their unique personality traits.

Why Choose Them

If you're looking for a rabbit that's both beautiful and kind-hearted, the New Zealand White is a perfect match. They're gentle, easy to care for, and have a calm, loving nature that makes them ideal companions for homes, schools, and small farms.

These rabbits are known for their intelligence and can be trained to use litter boxes, respond to their names, and even perform simple tricks. Their docile temperament makes them excellent therapy animals as well.

  • Friendly and sociable nature
  • Low maintenance compared to other pets
  • Long lifespan with proper care (5-10 years)
  • Great with children and other pets
  • Minimal grooming requirements

Care & Environment

Proper care is essential for keeping your rabbit healthy and happy. New Zealand Whites thrive in environments where they have space to hop and explore, along with a safe enclosure for resting.

Housing: Provide a spacious cage or hutch with plenty of room to move around. Include hiding spots and platforms for enrichment.

Diet: A balanced diet of hay, fresh vegetables, and high-quality pellets ensures optimal health. Fresh water should always be available.

Exercise: Daily supervised time outside their enclosure is crucial for physical and mental wellbeing.

Health: Regular veterinary check-ups and proper grooming maintain your rabbit's health and prevent common issues.
